About Our Mosque

Madeena Masjid in Dubauli village was established in 1985 to serve the growing Muslim community in the area. Over the years, it has become not just a place of worship but a center for Islamic education and community development.

Our mission is to provide a spiritual home for Muslims in the region, offering daily prayers, Friday congregations, Islamic classes for children and adults, and various community services.

The mosque has undergone several expansions to accommodate the growing number of worshippers and now features separate prayer areas for men and women, a madrasa, and a community center.

Our Services

Our Services

We provide various services to our community members

Daily Prayers

All five daily prayers are held in congregation at the mosque with Imam leading the prayers.

Quran Classes

Daily Quran memorization and recitation classes for children and adults of all ages.

Friday Khutbah

Weekly Friday prayer with khutbah in Urdu and Arabic, addressing contemporary issues.

Islamic Education

Comprehensive Islamic studies program covering Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah and Hadith.

Community Support

Marriage services, funeral arrangements, and counseling for community members.

Ramadan Services

Daily iftar during Ramadan, Taraweeh prayers, and special night programs.
